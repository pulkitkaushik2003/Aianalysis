Extraction tips

  • Keep the table straight and avoid extreme tilt.
  • Use a sharper image if the text looks blurry or low-contrast.
  • Make sure grid lines or column spacing are visible for best results.
  • For handwritten tables, clear lighting and dark ink help a lot.
